Certified Lover Data

GENRIFY.

Certified Lover Data mixes Billboard’s Hot 100 history with Spotify audio features to uncover what makes a song a hit. You’ll explore:

  • 👉 A “race” chart showing genres rising and falling over time
  • 👉 An interactive world map of each country’s top style
  • 👉 Heat-maps & scatter-plots revealing how danceability, energy, valence, tempo & popularity interact
  • 👉 A genre-prediction model from raw audio traits

Perfect for curious fans and music pros alike, it shows how genre, emotion, and geography shape today’s biggest hits.
